1. Introduction

Welcome to NeverForgotten Memorials ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data with care and respect. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our memorial creation and management services.

As an Ireland-based service, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws. By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Email address, password (hashed), name when you register
  • Memorial Content: Names, biographies, dates, photographs, stories, tributes, and other content you add to memorial pages
  • Payment Information: Billing details (processed securely by Stripe - we do not store credit card information)
  • Communications: Messages sent through our contact forms or support channels

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device information
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, memorial interactions, feature usage
  • Location Data: Approximate location derived from IP address (country/city level)

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use the following cookies:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Session management, security (CSRF protection), site functionality Session or up to 2 weeks
Analytics Cookies Google Analytics - understanding how visitors use our site Up to 2 years
Preference Cookies Remembering your cookie preferences 1 year
Cookie Consent: We only use non-essential cookies (like analytics) after obtaining your explicit consent through our cookie banner. Essential cookies for site functionality are always active.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To create, maintain, and display memorial pages as requested
  • To process payments and manage your subscription plan
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To improve our services and develop new features
  • To monitor and analyze usage patterns for service optimization
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security breaches
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under GDPR, we process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the memorial services you requested
  • Legitimate Interests: For service improvements, security, and analytics (with appropriate safeguards)
  • Consent: For non-essential cookies and optional features
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with Irish and EU laws

Data Transfers: Some services (like Stripe and Cloudinary) may store data outside the EU/EEA. We ensure they provide adequate safeguards through Standard Contractual Clauses or Privacy Shield certification.

6. Data Retention

  • Memorial Content: Retained indefinitely to preserve the memorial as requested, unless deletion is requested
  • User Accounts: Retained while active, deleted upon request or after 2 years of inactivity
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years as required by Irish tax law (processed by Stripe)
  • Server Logs: Retained for 30 days for security monitoring
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 26 months in Google Analytics

Upon account deletion, memorials will be anonymized or deleted based on your preference. Please note that public memorial content may remain in search engine caches beyond our control.

7. Your Data Protection Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

Right Description How to Exercise
Right to Access Receive a copy of your personal data Email request with verification
Right to Rectification Correct inaccurate or incomplete data Edit in account settings or email request
Right to Erasure Request deletion of your data Delete account in settings or email request
Right to Restriction Limit processing of your data Email request
Right to Data Portability Receive your data in a structured format Email request - we'll provide memorial data in JSON format
Right to Object Object to certain processing activities Email request or adjust cookie preferences
Right to Withdraw Consent Withdraw consent at any time Adjust settings or email request

Response Time: We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not specifically directed at children under 18. However, we acknowledge that:

  • Memorials may be created for children
  • Children may be included in memorial photographs
  • Young people may create accounts with parental consent

If you are under 18, please obtain parental consent before using our services. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with personal data without consent may contact us to request deletion.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular backups with encryption

Despite our efforts, no security system is impenetrable. In the event of a data breach that may risk your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by GDPR.

10. International Data Transfers

As an Ireland-based service with international users, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). We ensure such transfers comply with GDPR through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses with service providers
  • Privacy Shield certification (where applicable)
  • Binding Corporate Rules
  • Other approved transfer mechanisms

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with updated "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification to registered users
  • Displaying a notice on our website for 30 days

Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Memorial-Specific Considerations

Given the sensitive nature of our services, we acknowledge these special circumstances:

  • Multiple Contributors: Memorials may have multiple contributors; each controls their own content
  • Public Memorials: Some memorials are publicly accessible; consider privacy when adding content
  • Legacy Access: We have no mechanism for transferring memorial ownership upon death
  • Emotional Data: Memorial content is deeply personal; we handle it with extra care and respect
  • Right to be Forgotten Balance: We balance deletion requests with memorial preservation for other contributors
Important Notice About Lifetime Plans: Our "Lifetime" or "Eternal" plans provide permanent access for the operational lifetime of NeverForgotten services. While we are committed to long-term preservation, we cannot guarantee perpetual service. In the unlikely event of service discontinuation, we will provide at least 6 months notice and data export options.
